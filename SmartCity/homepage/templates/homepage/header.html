SmartCity!

Brisbane is the capital of and most populous city in the Australian state of Queensland, and the third most populous city in Australia. Brisbane's metropolitan area has a population of 2.4 million, and the South East Queensland region, centred on Brisbane, encompasses a population of more than 3.5 million. SmartCity is a not for profit that provides information about City of Brisbane for three specific user types, the Business User, Students and Tourists. Information provided is based on the account type of the user .
